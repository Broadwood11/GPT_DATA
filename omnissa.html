üß≠ IGEL: The Secure Endpoint OS Platform

IGEL is no longer a device brand. It is a software platform that transforms any compatible x86 device laptop, thin client, or PC into a secure, centrally managed workspace endpoint.

Think of IGEL as the hardened edge that completes Omnissa Zero Trust workspaces.

  • Replaces Windows at the endpoint with an Immutable Endpoint OS that is read only, lightweight, and built for remote access to VDI, DaaS, SaaS, or browser based workspaces.
  • Integrates directly with Omnissa Workspace ONE and Horizon Cloud for unified management, conditional access, and SSO.
  • Eliminates local risk no local apps, no stored data, and no patch chaos at the endpoint.
  • Extends hardware life by up to five years, cutting endpoint total cost of ownership by 60 to 70 percent.

For Omnissa sellers, IGEL makes every endpoint a secure access node into Workspace ONE and Horizon. No more fragile, costly, patch heavy PCs to support across fleets and sites.
