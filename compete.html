Common Citrix Objections & How to Counter

Keep this open during renewal calls.

“Citrix delivers the best protocol performance.”

Blast Extreme now equals or beats HDX in most real-world scenarios. IGEL OS optimizes codec handling and reduces bandwidth usage at the endpoint.

“We’re cloud-agnostic, VMware isn’t.”

Horizon supports Azure, AWS, and on-prem. The Horizon Control Plane provides a single policy and UX layer across clouds—Citrix still manages environments per site.

“We offer built-in session security.”

Omnissa extends protection beyond the session: device posture + identity + session trust. IGEL removes the local endpoint attack surface completely.

“Citrix is more mature.”

Citrix is older—but Omnissa inherits 20+ years of VMware EUC innovation and re-emerges focused on modern work. Customers get both maturity and agility.

“Migration is hard.”

The IGEL + Omnissa migration program automates assessment, conversion, and pilot rollout. Customers like Haaglanden Medical Center moved in weeks, not months.
